Mohandas College of Enginnering and Technology (MCET) is an engineering and technology college located at village Anad, Thiruvananthapuram district, Kerala, India. The college, which is affiliated to the KTU, was established in 2002.Mohandas College is a project of the Mohandas Group of Companies and is managed by the VN Gangadhara Panikker Memorial Trust. The college has recived ISO 9001:2008 certification.
